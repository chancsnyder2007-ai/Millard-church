About Us

Welcome to New Hope Methodist Church. We are a welcoming community focused on faith, fellowship, and serving others. We cherish a traditional, old-time church atmosphere—like the kind your grandparents would have loved—while still embracing and welcoming younger generations. At New Hope, we enjoy both classic hymns and newer worship music, and we are always excited to meet new people. Everyone is invited, and everyone is welcome here.
